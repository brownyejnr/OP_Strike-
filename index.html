⚡ Flash Loans

What is a Flash Loan? A flash loan lets you borrow any amount of assets without collateral — as long as you repay the principal plus a 0.09% fee within the same Bitcoin block.

Use Cases: Arbitrage between DEXes, collateral swaps, self-liquidation, yield farming strategies, and liquidating undercollateralised positions for a bonus.

How it works on OPNet: The StrikeLend contract sends you the requested amount, calls your callback contract, then verifies repayment. If repayment fails, the entire transaction reverts — so funds are always safe.

⚠️ Advanced Users Only. Flash loans require writing a callback contract. Connect wallet and use the SDK to interact with StrikeLend.flashLoan().
